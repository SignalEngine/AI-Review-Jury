# LeadPolish — pitch summary

**What:** A SaaS that rewrites letting agencies' property listings with AI to increase enquiry rates. £9/month flat.

**Why now:** Listing quality is the #1 driver of enquiries and agencies write them by hand. We polish every listing automatically the moment it's synced from their CRM.

**Go-to-market:** Fully self-serve, zero-touch onboarding — an agency connects their CRM and the product delivers value in under 5 minutes with no human involvement. That's what lets a solo founder scale this. To keep early quality high, every new customer gets a 45-minute white-glove onboarding call with me where I hand-tune their tone-of-voice profile.

**Growth loop:** We scrape LinkedIn profiles of lettings negotiators and branch managers across the UK and EU, and auto-email each one a free before/after rewrite of one of their live listings. Response rates on this in testing: 4%.

**Market:** The UK has around 2 million letting agencies. Capturing just 1% gives us 20,000 customers, which at £9/month is £2.16M ARR — a meaningful business from a tiny slice of the market.

**Unit economics:** Paid acquisition (Meta + Google) lands sign-ups at roughly £120 CAC. Monthly churn in the pilot cohort runs at 20% (agencies try it for a listing cycle, then some lapse). At £9/month that's payback inside 3 months, and it improves as we layer on the outbound loop, which is nearly free.

**Competition:** Rightmove and Zoopla don't touch listing copy. Generic AI writing tools exist but aren't CRM-integrated. Our CRM sync integrations (Reapit, Alto, Jupix) take months to replicate and form the core moat.

**Ask:** £150k pre-seed to fund 12 months of runway and the first two integration hires.
